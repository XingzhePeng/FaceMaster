FaceMaster is a research project by Frank. You can get started by uploading some photos with human faces. What we first do is to extract all the face information in your photos. And with the technology called face recognition, we are able to find out which faces are the same person. Finally we keep an N:N relationship between the photos and people inside them. That is, we not only know all the people inside any particular photo but also know all the photos that contain one particular person.
